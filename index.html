Welcome to DMM

  • Players: Add names to the Party list in the Players tab.
  • NPC Library: SRD monsters load automatically with all their attacks. Add custom NPCs manually.
  • NPCs: Save creatures in the NPC Library.
  • Ailments: Save status effects in the Ailment Library.
  • Encounters: Start new encounters and pull your saved data in.
  • Initiative: Tap the init box to enter a number. Combatants auto-sort. Tap ✎ to edit.
  • NPC Attacks: Attacks are loaded automatically from the SRD bestiary. Tap any attack button to roll damage.
  • Assign Ailments: Use the Ailment dropdown. Tapping rolls damage, subtracts HP, and ticks down rounds.
  • Health Tracking: Use -1 and -5 buttons. HP cannot drop below zero.
  • Defeat: 0 HP marks the NPC DEAD and tallies XP.

DMM is a PWA — install it on your home screen for an app-like experience with offline support.

iPhone / iPad (Safari):

  1. Tap the Share button (box with arrow) in Safari
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add

Android (Chrome):

  1. Tap the ⋮ menu in Chrome
  2. Tap Add to Home screen
  3. Tap Add
